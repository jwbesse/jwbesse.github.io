About Me

I am an innovation researcher and data scientist. This website presents some of my recent work.

Research

Scientometrics

Analyzing research trends and impact using scientometric techniques. Measuring citation networks, tracking institutional collaboration, and identifying emerging areas of innovation. 		Network Analysis

Mapping and examining complex networks to uncover hidden structures and patterns. Modeling research collaborations, policy networks, and information diffusion.
Statistical Benchmarking

Applying statistical benchmarking to compare research and innovation performance across institutions, regions, and programs. Creating quantitative indicators to assess research performance, and policy outcomes, and the effectiveness of funding programs. 		Social Media Analytics

Analyzing digital conversations to track public engagements with science and emerging technologies. Analyzing media content and sentiment, detecting misinformation, and measuring the influence of scientific and policy discussions online.
